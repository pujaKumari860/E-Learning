What you'll learn about this course

  • A Front-End Developer is responsible for crafting the visual and interactive parts of a website or web application — everything the user sees, clicks, and interacts with.
  • From layout and design to functionality and responsiveness, front-end developers bring digital experiences to life using code and creativity.
  • Front-End Developers are the architects of user experience. They take the creative designs from UI/UX teams and transform them into fully functioning, visually engaging websites and web applications.
  • As a front-end developer, your job isn’t just to write code, but to solve problems, optimize performance, and ensure that users enjoy a seamless, responsive experience—on every screen and every browser.
